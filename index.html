Our Story

Tradition, passion, and authentic Indian flavors

From Family Kitchen to Restaurant

Food Paglu began in 1995 when our founder, Raj Sharma, decided to share his family's treasured recipes with Delhi. What started as a small kitchen serving traditional vegetarian meals to neighbors has grown into a beloved culinary destination.

Today, we continue to honor Raj's vision by preparing every dish with the same care, authenticity, and love that made those early meals so special.
